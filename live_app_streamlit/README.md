A **teacher-friendly AI-powered gradebook** built with **Streamlit**, allowing educators to analyze student performance, identify at-risk students, and suggest interventions.

**Live App:** [Smart AI Gradebook on Hugging Face Spaces](https://huggingface.co/spaces/FariaNoumi09/gradebook)

---

## Features

- **Login System**: Simple username/password authentication.
- **Interactive EDA Tabs**: Overview, histograms, boxplots, correlation heatmaps, and pairplots.
- **Risk Assessment**: Automatically identifies at-risk students based on grades, study time, family support, and absences.
- **Machine Learning**: Random Forest model predicting at-risk students with performance metrics.
- **SHAP & LIME Explanations**: Visual interpretation of feature importance.
- **Teacher Smart Gradebook**: Suggested interventions and future support for students.
- **CSV Download**: Export the enhanced gradebook.
